A Quest For M'Lady

A top-down mobile adventure game with multiple endings

2016


A Quest for Mâ€™Lady is an action game where the player tries to rescue a princess. The player starts off as a child in daycare center. They must choose their father out of three candidates and the player stats are based on the father. After that the game progresses to adulthood and the player starts their rescue mission.

The game has puzzles, hiding, collectibles and a maze. The player has multiple choices of how to advance and the ending of the game depends on these choices. The game also has some humor elements, from dogs that tell random facts to the fact that the language of the game is Shakespearian English.

I was a part of creating A Quest for M'Lady as part of a group for a school project. My responsibilities were in design and programming.
