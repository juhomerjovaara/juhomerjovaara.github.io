Stone Age Sports

A comical online party game with wonky controls

2017-2018

Stone Age Sports is a fun multiplayer online party game where each limb is controlled separately creating a hilarious gaming experience. The goal is to defeat your friends in different sporting events while sabotaging their performance and having a lot of fun.

Events

Sprint

Players try to be the first to finish the race. The race is on a cliff. Players can tackle each other and are able to fall off the cliff.

Archery

Players try to shoot their own targets while trying to avoid shooting their opponents ones.

Boulder throw

Players try to throw a boulder as far as possible.

Long Jump

Players try to jump as far as possible.

Sumo

Players try to be the last man standing on a cliff. The cliff slowly gets smaller and smaller. Players can tackle each other out of the playing area.


I was a part of creating Stone Age Sports as a part of a group for a school project. My responsibilities were in 3D, design and programming.
