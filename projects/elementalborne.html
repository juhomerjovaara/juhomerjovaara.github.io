Elementalborne

Elementalborne is a co-op RPG with different kinds of enemies and puzzles

2018
Platform: PC

The game is for 1-3 players. The point of the game is to find keys by defeating enemies and solving puzzles. After finding all the keys in a level the player faces a boss battle.

Elementalborne was a school project made in a group. My responsibilities were in programming, level design and design.
