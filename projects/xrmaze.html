XRMaze

action game combining AR and VR

2019
Platform: PC (VR) and mobile (AR) at the same time

XRMaze is a school innovation project created for Helsinki XR Center that combines VR and AR

XRMaze is an online game where the AR (mobile) player creates a maze and places the VR (PC) player inside. The VR player tries to escape the maze and the AR player tries to block their exit by using tools such as a slingshot.


I was a part of creating XRMaze as a part of a group for a school innovation project. Helsinki XR Center was the customer. My responsibilities were in design and programming. VR and level generation were my main responsibilities in programming.
